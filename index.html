Data Analytics

What We Value

Growth Fundamentals
We pay close attention to intrinsic growth parameters that drive real estate markets - Supply and Demand. We understand that prices are fundamentally influenced by buyer activity as well as stock on the market, so we have developed an unparalleled methodology to analyse both sides of the equation.

Data and Statistical Accuracy

There's a problem with the real estate industry - if you've been around long enough, you would have noticed that the reported data never seems to be correct! How is it possible that some suburbs can report 40%+ growth in a month? The truth is - it typically isn't. Data is plagued in this industry by skewed data acquisition and interpretations. We have a commitment to set this straight with our proprietary algorithms.

Location, location and... what else?

In this industry, often you will hear spruiked that in property investing, location is everything. That simply isn't true - typically, you want to purchase the type of dwelling that is growing in preference amongst buyers. We have a commitment to analysing every property investing fundamental so that you can rest assured you are buying safely and optimally.

Economy and Infrastructure

Investing in the right area is much more than analysing the Supply and Demand dynamics - otherwise, we should all be investing in any buzzing new mining town and leave it at that! It's important to understand the local stability of the economy and incorporate future infrastructure plans and impacts, and our proprietary algorithms do exactly that.

Expert Analysis

Don't let anyone tell you differently - no amount of data or statistics can predict the future 100%. This is why understanding the plans of infrastructure growth and the economy is so important - we thus quality-check our predictions on expert economist and town planner review to ensure that the data is consistent with the more immeasurable aspects of the economy.
